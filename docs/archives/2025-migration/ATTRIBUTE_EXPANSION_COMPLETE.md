## Template System

### 10 Core Templates Used

1. **Brute** - High STR/CON, low DEX/INT (trolls, ogres)
2. **Agile** - High DEX, moderate other stats (rogues, hunters, flying enemies)
3. **Tank** - Very high CON, moderate STR (armored enemies, guards)
4. **Spellcaster INT** - High INT, low physical stats (wizards, sages, liches)
5. **Cleric WIS** - High WIS, moderate CON (healers, priests, shamans)
6. **Leader CHA** - High CHA, balanced other stats (nobles, commanders, vampires)
7. **Balanced** - All 12-14 (common humanoids, soldiers, craftsmen)
8. **Beast** - Low INT (3-5), high physical stats (animals, insects)
9. **Mindless** - Very low INT (1-2), moderate physical (zombies, skeletons)
10. **Dragon Epic** - All stats 18-26 (dragons, ancient creatures, epic bosses)

### Level Scaling

- **Low Tier (1-5)**: Total attributes 60-70
- **Mid Tier (6-10)**: Total attributes 70-80
- **High Tier (11-15)**: Total attributes 80-95
- **Epic Tier (16+)**: Total attributes 95-110+

## Usage in Gameplay

### Combat
- **STR**: Melee attack damage, carry capacity
- **DEX**: Ranged attack accuracy, initiative, dodge
- **CON**: HP total, endurance, resist poison/disease
- **INT**: Spell power (arcane), skill checks (investigation, appraisal)
- **WIS**: Spell power (divine), perception, insight
- **CHA**: Social interactions, leadership, charm effects

### Skill Checks
- Each attribute now provides modifiers for relevant skill checks
- NPCs with high INT (scholars, sages) excel at knowledge checks
- NPCs with high WIS (healers, priests) excel at perception and insight
- NPCs with high CHA (merchants, nobles) excel at persuasion
