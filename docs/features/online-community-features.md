# Online & Community Features

**Status**: See [IMPLEMENTATION_STATUS.md](../IMPLEMENTATION_STATUS.md)

## Overview

Community engagement through leaderboards, challenges, and shared content extends replayability and social interaction.

## Core Components

### Online Features
- **Global Leaderboards**: Rank characters across difficulty modes and playstyles
- **Daily Challenges**: Pre-generated challenges with unique rewards
- **Save Sharing**: Export/import characters and builds
- **Community Events**: Limited-time content and seasonal activities
- **Achievement Showcases**: Display accomplishments and compare with others

### Community Integration
- **Build Sharing**: Share character builds with community
- **Challenge Friends**: Send custom challenges to other players
- **Leaderboard Filters**: By class, difficulty, playstyle, time period
- **Event Participation**: Compete in limited-time events
- **Social Features**: Friend lists, messaging, guilds (optional)

## Key Features

- **Optional Connectivity**: Online features optional, offline fully functional
- **Competitive Element**: Leaderboards for competitive players
- **Cooperative Sharing**: Share knowledge and builds
- **Fresh Content**: Daily challenges provide new objectives
- **Community Driven**: Player-generated content and discoveries
