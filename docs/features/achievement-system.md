# Achievement System

**Status**: See [IMPLEMENTATION_STATUS.md](../IMPLEMENTATION_STATUS.md)

## Overview

The Achievement System tracks player milestones and accomplishments across playthroughs with rewards and persistence.

## Core Components

### Achievement Categories
Major milestone achievements tracking player accomplishments across multiple areas:
- **Combat Milestones**: First victories, boss defeats, combat mastery
- **Progression Milestones**: Level thresholds, skill mastery, power achievements
- **Exploration Milestones**: Location discovery, world exploration completion
- **Economic Milestones**: Wealth accumulation, trading success
- **Quest Milestones**: Story completion, quest chain achievements
- **Challenge Milestones**: Difficulty completions, special objectives

### Achievement Features
- **Persistence**: Achievements retained across saves and New Game+
- **Meaningful Rewards**: Unlocks, bonuses, titles, cosmetic recognition, and content access
- **Progress Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of achievement progress
- **Completion Notifications**: Visual feedback on unlock
- **Variety**: Mix of straightforward and hidden/secret achievements

## Key Features

- **Milestone Recognition**: Celebrate player accomplishments across all game systems
- **Replay Value**: Completionists encouraged to unlock all achievements
- **Tangible Rewards**: Unlocks provide mechanical benefits, not just cosmetic recognition
- **Cross-Playthrough**: Achievements persist through multiple runs and New Game+
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Mix of easy early achievements and challenging endgame goals
- **Discovery Element**: Some achievements hidden until unlocked for surprise moments
